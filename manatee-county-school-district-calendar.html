About

The Manatee County School District is a public school district located in Manatee County, Florida, United States. It serves the educational needs of approximately 48,000 students across 62 schools.

The district’s mission is to educate and develop all students academically, socially, and emotionally so that they may acquire the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in a changing world.

The Manatee County School District offers a range of programs and services for its students, including advanced placement courses, dual enrollment opportunities, career and technical education, and exceptional student education. The district also provides various extracurricular activities and athletics programs for its students.

The Manatee County School District is governed by an elected school board, which oversees the district’s policies, budget, and operations. The district has a superintendent who is responsible for the day-to-day management of the district.
