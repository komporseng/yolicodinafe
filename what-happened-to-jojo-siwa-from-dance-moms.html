What Happened to JoJo Siwa from Dance Moms?

June 2024 Â· 3 minute read

JoJo Siwa, originally known as Joelle Joanie, first gained public recognition at a tender age of 9 when she participated in the reality TV show "Dance Moms."

Since then, her journey has been nothing short of remarkable, marked by a series of career milestones, personal revelations, and notable achievements.

Rising to Stardom on Dance Moms

JoJo Siwa's journey to fame began with her participation in the Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition, a spinoff of Dance Moms, during its second season in 2013.

Her infectious energy and larger-than-life personality quickly captured the audience's attention, leading to her subsequent appearance on the fifth and sixth seasons of Dance Moms, alongside her mother, Jessalyn Siwa.

Career Progression and Notable Achievements

Following her stint on "Dance Moms," JoJo Siwa's career skyrocketed. She ventured into various creative endeavors, establishing herself as a multi-talented personality. Some of her career highlights include:

  • Social Media Sensation: Siwa's social media following exploded post-"Dance Moms," with millions of followers on platforms like TikTok and Instagram.
  • Merchandising Success: Siwa's signature accessory, big, colorful hair bows, became a trademark, leading to lucrative merchandise deals with major brands such as Claire's and Nickelodeon.
  • Television Appearances: She ventured into hosting successful tours, launching merchandise lines, and appearing on multiple reality TV shows, including "So You Think You Can Dance" and "Dancing with the Stars," where she made history as the first contestant with a same-sex dance partner.
  • Musical Endeavors: Siwa delved into music, releasing songs and music videos, with her track "Karma" garnering attention in April 2024.

Personal Life and Transformation

Apart from her professional achievements, JoJo Siwa has been open about her personal life, including her journey of self-discovery and coming out as a member of the LGBTQ+ community in 2021. 

Her decision to share her authentic self with the world has made her an inspiring role model for many, especially for her courage and authenticity in navigating her personal identity.

Significant Events and Transformations

Siwa's journey has seen significant transformations, both professionally and personally. Notable events include:

  • Public Coming Out: Siwa's public coming out in 2021 marked a pivotal moment in her life and career, and she has since been an advocate for self-acceptance and inclusivity.
  • Relationship Trauma: In a candid discussion on the "Call Her Daddy" podcast, Siwa revealed the challenges she faced in a past relationship, emphasizing the impact it had on her and her approach to future relationships.

JoJo Siwa's post-"Dance Moms" trajectory has been an inspiring saga of talent, resilience, and authenticity.

From a young reality star to a multifaceted influencer, Siwa has not only expanded her career horizons but also embraced her true self, becoming a beacon of empowerment and acceptance for her millions of fans.

As she continues to evolve and make her mark in various industries, her journey remains a testament to the power of staying true to oneself amidst the spotlight.
