YG, Saweetie &amp; Tyga To Embark On Str8 To The Klub Tour

July 2024 · 3 minute read

YG, Saweetie and Tyga have announced that they will soon hit the road together as part of their upcoming Str8 to the Klub Tour, which begins in the fall.

The 14-date run kicks off on September 21 in Sacramento and wraps up on November 21 in Los Angeles, with stops in cities such as San Francisco, Las Vegas, Vancouver, Dallas and San Diego along the way.

Kamiyah, Wallie The Sensei, and DJ Vision will serve as openers on the tour. Tickets go on sale Friday (July 21) at 10 a.m. local time via YG’s website.

According to TMZ, YG and Saweetie — who began dating earlier this year — also have new music in the works together, with a collaboration set to drop on July 28.

AD LOADING...

The outlet also reported that the Hip Hop power couple are “actively working on new music together, possibly even an EP, and will even be dropping a new video soon.”

The Compton rapper is also rumored to be cooking up a full-length collaboration with Tyga. The pair have dropped two singles together in recent months with “Platinum” and the Blxst-assisted “West Coast Weekend.”

The Str8 to the Klub Tour will serve as YG’s second headlining tour this year following his 2023 Red Cup Tour, and will be the first major public outing for YG and Saweetie.

The pair confirmed dating rumors in May when they were spotted packing on the PDA during a romantic vacation in Mexico.

AD LOADING...

Speculation began swirling the previous month after the duo were spotted “getting cozy” at a California restaurant during Coachella weekend.

Saweetie also stopped by YG’s 33rd birthday party in West Hollywood back in March alongside other celebs like Travis Scott, Offset, Mustard, Ty Dolla $ign and A$AP Ferg.
