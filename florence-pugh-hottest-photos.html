Florence Pughs Hottest Photos Hollywood Life

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Florence Pugh is one of Hollywood’s hottest young stars! Here are some of the best photos of the British actress over the years.

Florence Pugh has been turning heads ever since she came onto the Hollywood scene with her acting debut in 2014 for the drama film The Falling. Her unique look and savvy style were all the rage when she dared to impress on the red carpet for her turns as professional wrestler Paige in the sports film Fighting with My Family, a terrified woman in the horror film Midsommar, and Amy March in the period drama Little Women.

Even after an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress for Little Women, Florence didn’t become a household name until she landed herself a role in a Marvel superhero film. Playing the younger sister of Scarlett Johansson’s character in Black Widow, Florence cemented herself as a bona fide movie star. And her flawless fashion choices promoting the film caused her to be a sensation with the shutterbugs as well! Scroll through to see some more of the young star’s hottest looks!
