Leo Svete On Are You The One?

The wait is over! Are You The One? is back for its ninth installment after a four-year hiatus. Once again, 22 contestants from around the world are coming together to find their compatible half, and our star, Leo Svete is one of them.

“Last year when I had nowhere turn going through tough times in my life, I took a chance at finding love and hoping to win 1 million dollars,” Leo talked a little about why he joined the show.

For those whose memory needs a jog (or if you’re completely new to the show), every contestant on the show must go through extensive interviews and compatibility tests before filming. Thereafter, the show uses a special matchmaking algorithm tool to ensure all contestants have a “perfect match.” So, the goal is to figure out who they are destined for through a “truth booth.”

If the group can figure out every true match in 10 or fewer attempts, they get to split a $1,000,00 prize. However, each time the contestants fail to figure out any new matches, the winnings are cut in half.

Leo Svete Relationship Status

Leo Svete claimed to be “single” as of 2023. Or at least that was what his FB profile mentioned. So, could this mean that Leo and Brooke Rachman didn’t make it out of the show?

And that was Leo!

On the other hand, Will said he felt like he was only Brooke’s shoulder to cry on when Leo wasn’t giving her attention.
