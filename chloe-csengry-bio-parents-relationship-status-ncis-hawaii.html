Meet Actress Chloe Csengry! Her Bio, Parents, Relationship Status, NCIS

June 2024 · 4 minute read

The Season 1 of CBS spinoff, NCIS: Hawai’i kicked off in November 2021 and introduced viewers to Special Agent in Charge Jane Tennant (actress Vanessa Lachey) and her team of special agents. Chloe Csengry, whom we are going to discuss in the rest of the writing, however, joined the cast midway.

And now we have been told to inform you all about her in this ‘Chloe Csengry BIO’.

Chloe Csengry On NCIS: Hawai’i

Ahead of her episode on NCIS: Hawai’i went on air, Chloe Csengry teased her appearance on the series. She was finally allowed to disclose “Joining the NCIS: Hawai’i fam tonight 10/9c on CBS! Can’t wait for everyone to meet Gracie Boone!” in a social media post.

Viewers get acquainted with Chloe’s character Gracie Boone in S01 E15 of NCIS: Hawai’i.

Gracie, here, is the daughter of NCIS Field Agent Jesse Boone (actor Noah Mills), who is Tennant’s confidant and second-in-command.

In an attempt to bond well with his daughter the former homicide detective in Washington, DC, books a day of sailing lessons out on the water. He even pleads with Gracie to accompany him on a six-week sailing trip than to rather go camping with her friends. But their argument is put on hold as pirates suddenly seize their yacht, take all the passengers hostage, and attack Jesse, leaving it up to his team to locate and rescue his dear daughter.

The 7 March 2022-episode was the first time NCIS: Hawai’i had gone personal about Jesse.

We will have to tune into next the next (Monday) episode on CBS to see if the father-daughter duo makes it home safe.

Who Are Chloe Csengry Parents?

Chloe’s mother is Stacey Harvey Csengery who turned 52 in 2021. As per LinkedIn, Stacey is a co-owner at Greater Houston-based Amazing Lash Kingwood. You could find her on Instagram at @harv033.

Then, there’s Chris Csengery, Chloe’s father, 78 years old as of 2021. He graduated from Kingwood High School, Baylor University, and Baylor College of Dentistry in 1996 and purchased an existing dental practice in the area.

The up-and-coming actress began taking acting classes at a very young age, thanks to her parents who recognized the performer in her that early on.

Chloe Csengry Relationship Status

Chloe Csengry was yet to update her relationship status on Facebook as of the time of this writing. Although back in the prom days (2017), she seemed to be dating Brandon Ross, likely her then classmate. One time, Chloe even had admitted that their relationship started on Instagram’s ‘Direct Message’.

As per Facebook, Brandon, here, left Kingwood High School in 2018 and then attended the University of Mississippi.

Fast forward to today, Chloe seemed rather occupied by her aspiration to make it big in the industry. Having just completed her studies, she has a long way to go. So, fans maybe have to wait for some time before she introduces to them her love story.

Chloe Csengry Height

Chloe Csengry stands 5′ 1″ (1.55 meters) tall.

How Old Is Chloe Csengry?

Chloe was born on 7 July 2000. That means she is a Cancer who turned 21 in 2021.

How Much Is Chloe Csengry Net Worth?

Chloe Csengry reportedly owned around $500K as net worth as of March 2022.

Before joining the cast of NCIS: Hawai’i, she had guest roles in Criminal Minds, Parenthood, Law & Order: SVU, Modern Family, Chicago Med, The Good Doctor, and Paranormal Activity 3. Chloe got to share the screen with Christina Applegate in Up All Night.

Over the course, she is best known for her role in Paranormal Activity. She has been seen in more than one of the Paranormal Activity films.

So, Chloe, who also notably played Maisie, a one-time character in Modern Family, wittily describes her profession, as “I pretend to be other people for a living.”
