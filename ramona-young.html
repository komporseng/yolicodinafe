Career

In Films

She made her acting debut with the role of ‘Eve/Ghost’ with the film Specter (2010). In 2015, she made her debut as a director, writer, and producer with the film, Live Exit Here; she also acted in the film. She has worked in many short films like Twelve (2015), Wanderers (2015), Reflections (2016; also produced and wrote), Star Crossed (2016; also wrote), Harry Decision (2016; also cinematographer), Date Night (2016), Amuse’d (2017), and Bottomland (2018). She has also acted in many full-length films such as Thinning (2016), Blockers (2018), All About Nina (2018), and Unpregnant.

In Television

She made her television debut with the show, This Indie Thing (2012) as ‘Kevin’s Rude Fan.’ She has appeared in many memorable roles in the popular series, Super Fun Night (2014), The Real O’Neals (2016-17), Z Nation (2016-18) as ‘Kaya,’ Santa Clarita Diet (2017-19), Review (2017), Legends of Tomorrow (2018), and Never Have I Ever (2020) as ‘Eleanor.’
